How to Play

  1. Select your civilization and start the game
  2. Gather resources with villagers and build structures
  3. Train an army and research technologies
  4. Advance through ages to unlock more powerful units
  5. Achieve victory by destroying enemies or amassing wealth!

Quick Tutorial

1
Gather Resources

Select villagers and right-click on resource nodes to gather wood, food, gold, stone, or iron.

2
Build Structures

Click on the Build button, select a structure, and place it on the map. Villagers will construct it.

3
Train Units

Click on barracks or other military buildings to train soldiers, siege units, and heroes.

4
Research Technologies

Open the Tech Tree tab to research technologies that enhance your civilization.

5
Age Advancement

Build required structures and gather resources to advance to the next age.
